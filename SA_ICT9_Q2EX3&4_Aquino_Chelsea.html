Last Night At The Telegraph Club (Paperback) by Malindo Lo

The time 1954 isn't exactly a safe place for two women to fall in love, especially not in Chinatown. Red-Scare paranoia threatens everyone, including Chinese Americans like Lily. With deportation looming over her father despite his hard won citizenship, Lily and Kath risk everything to let their love see the light of day.
